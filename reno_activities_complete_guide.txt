SAFETY TIPS
-----------
- Check weather before heading out
- Bring plenty of water (high altitude = faster dehydration)
- Use sun protection (elevation increases UV exposure)
- Tell someone your plans
- Check fire restrictions in summer
- Be aware of altitude sickness above 8,000 feet
- Watch for afternoon thunderstorms in summer
- Check avalanche conditions in winter
