SUMMER SAFETY

Altitude Sickness Prevention
- Hydrate well (drink 2-3x normal water)
- Ascend gradually
- Rest if symptoms develop

Sun Protection
- Use SPF 50+ sunscreen
- Wear hat and sunglasses
- UV exposure increases with altitude

Thunderstorm Safety
- Watch for afternoon storms (typically 2-5pm)
- Get below treeline if thunder heard
- Avoid exposed ridges and summits

Wildlife
- Bear country: store food properly
- Keep dogs leashed
- Never feed wildlife
